# Insurance Claims and Policyholder Risk Analysis using Power BI

![](https://your-repo-link/logo.png)

## Overview  
This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of insurance claims and policyholder risk using Power BI. The dashboard visualizes key metrics such as claim frequency, average claim amount, and policyholder demographics to support data-driven decisions in underwriting, risk assessment, and customer segmentation.

## Objectives

- Analyze total claims, claim frequency, and average claim amounts.
- Segment policyholders by age group and policy type.
- Visualize trends in claims and risk over time.
- Identify high-risk policy types and age groups.
- Build an interactive dashboard for insurance stakeholders.

## Dataset

The dataset includes policyholder and claim-level data with fields such as:

- **Fields Included:** Policy Type, Age Group, Claim Status, Claim Amount, Claim Frequency, Total Claims, Total Policyholders

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Top-Level KPIs  
**Objective:** Display key metrics at a glance.  
**Visuals:** Total Claims, Claim Frequency, Total Policyholders, Average Claim Amount.

### 2. Claim Frequency by Policy Type  
**Objective:** Identify which policy types have the highest claim rates.  
**Visualization:** Donut chart.

### 3. Policyholder Demographics  
**Objective:** Analyze distribution of policyholders by age group.  
**Visualization:** Bar chart.

### 4. Claim Frequency Trend  
**Objective:** Monitor changes in claim frequency over time.  
**Visualization:** Line chart.

### 5. Average Claim Amount by Policy Type  
**Objective:** Compare average claim amounts across policy types.  
**Visualization:** Bar chart.

### 6. Risk by Age Group  
**Objective:** Evaluate claim frequency and average claim amount by age.  
**Visualizations:** Line charts for frequency and amount trends.

### 7. Interactive Filtering  
**Objective:** Enable dynamic exploration of data.  
**Feature:** Filters for Policy Type, Age Group, and Claim Status.

## Findings and Conclusion

- **High-Risk Segments:** Certain policy types and age groups show elevated claim frequency.
- **Claim Trends:** Frequency and average amounts fluctuate seasonally and by demographic.
- **Policy Insights:** Visual comparisons help identify cost-heavy policies and optimize risk models.

This Power BI dashboard empowers insurance analysts to assess risk, refine policy offerings, and improve claims management through data visualization and segmentation.
